Triforge TD

Godot 4, GDScript, PostgreSQL, AWS Lambda, Firebase, AdMob

Mobile-first 3D tower defense with merge-3 upgrading, 6 hero classes, and gacha progression. 8 tower types with ~86 augments, 17 enemy types across 63 campaign levels, world boss & challenge modes. Server-authoritative gacha via AWS Lambda + Supabase, Firebase Auth, Apple IAP + Google Play Billing, offline-first architecture, and full CI/CD with CDK infrastructure-as-code.
